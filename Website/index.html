What Is CBD?
CBD is short for cannabidiol. It is one of many different molecules called cannabinoids that are found in the cannabis plant. You may have heard of THC, THC is the psychoactive ingredient in cannabis. CBD does NOT get you high, THC does.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Tincture?

Our tinctures are concentrated liquid CBD hemp extracts. The tinctures act as droppers to apply to your tongue or put on your food or in a beverage.

What if I have additional questions?

Not a problem! Our Tropica Organics team are experts on CBD, just reach out to us at support@tropicaorganics.com and we would be more than happy to answer any questions.

What are 'lab results'?

Check the bottom of your product bottle, where you'll find the product's lot number. Find this number under the appropriate product listed below and click on the link to view the lab results. The lab results contain information about the date of manufacture, attributes (like consistency and color), potencies, terpenes, pesticides, heavy metals, solvents and so on.
